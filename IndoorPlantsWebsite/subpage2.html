Essential Care Tips for Indoor Plants

Keep your indoor plants healthy by following these basic care tips:

  • Watering: Check soil moisture before watering. Most plants need water every 1-2 weeks.
  • Light: Ensure plants receive adequate light; use grow lights if necessary.
  • Humidity: Certain plants, like ferns, require higher humidity. Use a humidifier if needed.
