Top Indoor Plants for Health and Air Quality

Choosing the right indoor plants can transform your space. Consider these options for health and ease of care:

  • Snake Plant - Great for improving air quality and very low-maintenance.
  • Peace Lily - Perfect for low-light areas, helps purify air.
  • Pothos - Excellent for enhancing humidity and easy to grow.
